Monaco

By Sreyas P

The Principality of Monaco is a sovereign city-state on the French Riviera, renowned as the world's second-smallest independent nation and a premier destination for luxury, tourism, and banking. Ruled by the House of Grimaldi since 1297, this densely populated, tax-haven principality is famous for the glamorous Monte-Carlo casino, the annual Formula 1 Grand Prix, and its mild Mediterranean climate.
